About Us

We are an AI-enabled marketplace connecting Brands with the Influencers. A database that offers Region, Reach and Engagement, further delivering highly efficient influencer marketing campaigns that create an engaging hubbub among the consumers. We have access to over 1000 influencers from all over india, spread across various categories varying from lifestyle, Comedy, food, travel and many more. With the use of Artificial Intelligence, we analyse these influencers reach based on the brand's needs, further creating customised and highly efficient marketing campaigns for our clients.

Youtube and Instagram are the 2 mediums we currently operate with, our platform offers complete analysis of your campaigns and summarises them with a report combining total reach, influencers you've worked with, total spend, regional data.

Database

Define your needs on the basis of estimated budget, reach, category and language. We have over 10 categories of influencers to choose from, operating in different languages varying from tamil, gujrati, haryanvi, malayalam, kannada and many more.

We prepare a list of influencers based on your requirement, who would be able to advertise the product in the most appropriate manner further maximising the reach and engagement of your campaign. Also specifying an estimated spend and the promotion type.

Moving on well come up with a plan of action integrating our delivery plan and the marketing strategy to create an efficient and impactful influencer marketing campaign. Strategies can vary from a video integration type campaign to a pre roll, from a product promotion to a service one, weve got it all covered.

Implementing the strategy with the chosen influencers, sharing campaign briefs, approving advertisement clips, sharing tracking links and finally going live. We make it all look simple !

We offer a dynamic dashboard which reflects your current campaign status, bringing in the tracking links, video links, spend, views all in one place making this dashboard one of its kind. It also analyses your campaign based on the videos published by the creators, further identifying what worked and what didn't, then suggesting tips for future campaigns.
